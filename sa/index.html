Discover my interests and hobbies

My Football Journey

I began football training with FCBescola, FC Barcelona’s Moroccan affiliate, spending two formative years there. During this time, I attended the Nike Football Camp in Andorra, which sharpened my skills, and visited Camp Nou with my team, deepening my passion for the sport.

After FCBescola, I played as a goalkeeper for three years with Oasis City Sport, developing focus, agility, and decision, making skills valuable both in sports and professionally. Despite an injury in 2019, I remain committed and still enjoy playing with friends.

My Archery Passion

My passion for archery began with the TV show Arrow, which sparked my initial fascination with the skill and precision required in the sport. I practiced independently for about a year, focusing on technique and control. Over time, I became more intrigued by the physics involved forces, motion, and trajectory, which led me to study these principles more deeply during my preparatory classes (CPGE). This deeper exploration helped me appreciate the scientific foundation of archery and how it connects with my broader interests in engineering and physics.

My Love of Books

My love for books began with tales of Greek and Norse mythology, especially through the journeys of Percy Jackson and Magnus Chase. These epics sparked my imagination, leading me to explore manga like Vagabond and One Piece, which opened up new worlds and storytelling styles. Later, I became drawn to philosophical works, particularly those by Hannah Arendt, for her insights on society and human nature. Today, I'm captivated by Alexandre Dumas, especially Les Trois Mousquetaires and Le Chevalier de la Maison Rouge, whose rich narratives and complex characters have become an inspiring obsession.

My DJ discovery

I discovered DJing through CAN7, the party club at ENSEEIHT, which opened my eyes to a completely new way of experiencing music. Being part of this community gave me a chance to dive into the art of mixing and shaping the vibe of an entire room. DJing transformed how I listen to music, it’s no longer just about the melodies or lyrics but also about understanding rhythms, transitions, and how each track can bring energy and emotion to a crowd. This experience has been an exciting journey of discovery and creativity.
